Biography

My name is Gasenia Caraballo, but I prefer to be called Gia. I was born in Syracuse, New York, to Sharon Frazier and Gustavo Caraballo. I am the youngest of four. I was the only girl out of the crew of boys. Therefore, I had a point to prove. I have always been a scholar. During my school-age years, I was heavily active in my community. This inspired my interest in wanting to help others, knowing that education was the key to my success. I continued on the path to make a difference. I graduated from Miami Dade College with my Associate’s degree. By 2026, I will have earned my Bachelor of Science in Information Technology from Florida A & M University. I am a systems engineer and lead scheduler at US Southern Command for the Collaboration Section. Additionally, I am the President of Gia Del Rose Inc. It is a marketing and branding company. I am also the Chief of Operations and Director of Modeling for C Smith Modeling LLC. I aspire to become a mogul for my family and friends to look up to.
